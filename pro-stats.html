Mastering the "Walls"

Don't waste progression points. Our data shows that attributes hit "Soft Walls" at 85 and "Hard Walls" at 90.

  • 85 Wall: The baseline for competitive online play. Anything lower results in heavy touches.
  • 90 Wall: The "Elite" tier. This is where player unique IDs (like Messi or Neymar) perform best.
  • 95+ Wall: Extreme diminishing returns. Only use for your primary striker's finishing or a winger's speed.
